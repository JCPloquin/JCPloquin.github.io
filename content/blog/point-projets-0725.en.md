## Hydrophonia – Remix 2025  
The 9-minute stereo version of *Hydrophonia – Remix 2025* is now available [on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mFXsNs8Gd4Y).  
At the same time, the piece has been submitted to **PAYSAGES | COMPOSÉS 2025**, the call for experimental sound works organised by **APNÉES**. Jury results are expected at the end of August.

## Volume 4 of *From Death to Life*  
Writing continues: the manuscript has just passed the 7,000-word mark.  
The **title is still to be decided**, but the overall structure is locked in:  
- novella format, like the first three volumes (≈32,000 words)  
- 3 main acts broken into roughly ten chapters  
- several interludes titled “Voices of Homo Permanens”  
Target for the first full read-through: **October 2025**.

## Game – Fluctus Planetarium  
The game adaptation of **“Fluctus Planetarium”** has entered the polishing phase:  
- event-card balancing was completed last week  
- rulebook revision now at v1.12.3  
- prototypes printed for early-August blind-play tests  

> A **stable release** is pencilled in for **September 2025**, provided the final tests go smoothly.
