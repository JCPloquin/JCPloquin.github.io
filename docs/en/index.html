Hydrophonia

My latest electroacoustic poem, created for the 2023 Nautil’Art Festival at the Halle aux Toiles in Rouen.

This 60-minute cyclical composition explores the aquatic element through four looping movements with no defined beginning or end.

I. Aquastasis - The primordial aquatic balance
II. Cosmogenesis - The cosmic birth of waters
III. Exolvuntur - The evolution of aquatic forms
IV. Fons Vitae - The universal source of life
